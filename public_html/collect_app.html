An all-in-one iPhone App for field collection notes

Take less into the field

Standard loadout includes a notebook, pencil, GPS unit, camera, vial labels, and often your phone.
Combine it all in one application.

More data with less hassel

Collect all the minium collection event data you need with one tap, plus more if you want.
All data fields are organized in a format ready for your database.
Have images of your collecting event attached to the record.

Fewer errors with greater efficiency

No more copying from your GPS to your notebook and again to your database.
Update your collector code sequentially.
Prelabel your vials with a barcode, and use the scanner feature to link the vial to a collecting event id number.
